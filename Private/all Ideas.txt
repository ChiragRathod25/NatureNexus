   - Create a platform for tracking and reporting wildlife sightings, which can be used by researchers and conservationists.

2. **Sustainable Agriculture and Food Systems**
   - Design a system that uses **IoT devices** for precision farming, such as soil moisture sensors and automated irrigation systems.

3. **Conservation Technology**
   - Use **remote sensing** data to monitor deforestation or illegal fishing activities in real-time.
   - Develop a machine learning model to predict areas of high biodiversity or endangered species habitats based on environmental data.

4. **Environmental Education and Awareness**
   - Create an interactive web platform with games and quizzes to educate users about environmental issues.
   - Develop a virtual reality experience that shows the impacts of climate change or habitat loss.

5. **Climate Change Mitigation and Adaptation**
   - Design a tool that calculates a user's carbon footprint based on their daily activities and suggests ways to reduce it.
   - Develop a predictive model that forecasts the impacts of climate change on local weather patterns, agriculture, and biodiversity.
